Beyond the Horizon

A Love Novel

By Walusimbi Leon (SGSS)

About

Beyond the Horizon is a sweeping love story about two people from different worlds who discover that distance is not measured in miles but in the willingness to close them. It is a story about the architecture of longing, the music of absence, and the courage it takes to step beyond the familiar.

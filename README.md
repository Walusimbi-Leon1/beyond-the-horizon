# 🌅 Beyond the Horizon

**A Love Novel by Walusimbi Leon (SGSS)**

---

## 📖 About

*Beyond the Horizon* is a sweeping, international love story about two people from different worlds who discover that distance is not measured in miles but in the willingness to close them. Set across Paris, Tokyo, New York, Bogotá, and Osaka, it is a story for anyone who has ever loved someone across a distance.
